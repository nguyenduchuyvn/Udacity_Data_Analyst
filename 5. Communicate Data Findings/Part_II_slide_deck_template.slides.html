Dataset Overview¶

his dataset is financial dataset and this is related to the loan, borrowers, lenders, interest rates... Prosper or Prosper Marketplace Inc. is a San Francisco, California based company specializing in loans at low interest rates to the borrowers. The dataset is comprised of 81 variables and contains 113937 entries.
